## [Introduction](#introduction)

Your Markdown is only as good as your typography.

This post showcases every Markdown style implemented via your custom
Tailwind utilities including headings, lists, tables, code blocks,
quotes, links, kbd elements, figures, and more.

If you're designing a content-heavy site, this typography layer is the
backbone of readability and polish.
